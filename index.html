About Beat Zen

The name Beat Zen was chosen to balance high-quality music. "Beat" refers to the heart of the songs we provide, while "Zen" represents the simple and focused features of our website. By using basic web technology, we ensure a smooth experience on all your devices so you can focus entirely on the music.

Top Features

  • Yearly Organization: Browse movie albums neatly grouped by their release year.
  • Instant Search: A fast search bar helps you find albums, songs, years, artists, or playlists in a blink.
  • Artists & explore: Discover your favorite singers and explore unique music explore.
  • Infinite Loop: Turn on the repeat button to keep your favorite song playing as many times as you listen.
  • Maximized Player: Simply tap the album cover or the player bar to enter a full-screen music experience.
  • Full Screen Info: The maximize view shows you the movie name and the playlist location clearly at the top.
  • Smart Sleep Timer: Set a timer for up to 24 hours with cool color animations that turn red as time runs out.
  • Direct Contact: Get direct contact with Website Creator using contact form for giving feedback or for asking to add any missing albums/songs in website; add your number for a quick WhatsApp reply.
